Entropy Prime

A zero-trust behavioral biometrics engine that moves beyond cookies and browser fingerprints to biological-physics security. Captures neuromuscular jitter and keystroke dwell/flight times — a 1D-CNN running entirely in-browser computes a humanity score (θ). A DQN agent then dynamically tunes Argon2id hashing strength per login. Bots scoring θ < 0.1 get silently redirected into a honeypot for threat intelligence. A deep autoencoder continuously anchors session trust and flags behavioral drift.
